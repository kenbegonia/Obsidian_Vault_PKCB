Tags: #habits #projectlockedin #self-improvement

---

## Glossary for keywords
- `Hexis` - a stable state wherein one has practiced one thing enough times that it became second nature to a person
- `Prohairesis` - the deliberate moral choice; an intentional, mindful decision of choosing actions and not because of habit or impulses

## Notes
- Aristotle believes that the slow accumulation of tendencies on approaching a certain thing is key to moral development

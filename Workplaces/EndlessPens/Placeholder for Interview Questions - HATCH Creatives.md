# Bio

```
Hi, my name is Teresa. I visited a fountain pen store for the first time; three years ago and I was dazzled by the beauty and variety of the pens. Also, I was totally out of my depth about pen brands, design, and nibs. On that day, my daughter purchased a pen for me; my first fountain pen; which was an Esterbrook. Shortly after that, she and I attended our first pen show in Atlanta. I purchased a Mythic Pens' creation there, that spiraled me down the rabbit hole of being a pen hobbyist. My modest YouTube channel; Travel By Books; which previously focused on book unboxings, became a fountain pen review channel and eventually gained momentum and subscribers, for whom I am eternally grateful. I owe a debt of gratitude for my daughter's guidance during my newbi days, and for that first pen, and to the many pen turners, blank makers, and fellow YouTube pen reviewers who have provided answers to my many questions and have offered support. My gratitude is endless. Thank you!
```

# April 2026 insights
- Overall Shopify email subscribers were reduced by 0.04% (not too significant a decrease), but showed a bit of growth on tiered subscribers for April, Platinum subscribers remained the same against March
- Overall Omnisend subscribers were reduced by 0.02% (not too significant a decrease), but showed a bit of growth on tiered subscribers for April
- Social media showed a bit of growth for Meta platforms; YouTube had a small decrease in subscriber count and a significant decrease in Reddit's weekly visitors for April
- Financials showed mixed indices; net sales and sales from first-time customers were decreased for April, while profits, conversion rates, and sales from return customers have increased

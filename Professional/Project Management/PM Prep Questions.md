# eCommerce Project Manager – Interview Question Bank

--- 

## Section A: Initial Interview (Screening / First Round)


### Background & Fit
- Can you tell us about your background and how it relates to eCommerce project management?
- What interested you in this role and our company?
- How would you describe your experience working on website or eCommerce projects?
- What types of teams have you worked with most closely?
- What do you consider your strongest skill as a project manager?  

### High-Level Project Experience
- Can you briefly walk us through an eCommerce or website project you worked on?
- What was your role from start to finish?
- How do you usually kick off a new project?
- What tools have you used to track project progress?
- How familiar are you with Agile or Scrum environments?

### Communication & Collaboration
- How do you typically communicate with developers, designers, and stakeholders?
- How do you ensure everyone stays aligned on priorities?
- How do you handle unclear or changing requirements early in a project?
- How comfortable are you speaking with non-technical stakeholders?

### Problem Awareness (Light Scenarios)
- What do you do if a task is taking longer than expected?
- How do you handle competing priorities from different stakeholders?
- How do you approach risk before it becomes a problem?

### eCommerce & Technical Awareness (High-Level)
- What eCommerce platforms have you worked with or are familiar with?
- What do you think are common challenges in eCommerce website projects?
- How do you ensure quality before a website goes live?

### Wrap-Up Questions
- What does success look like for you in the first 90 days?
- How do you like to receive feedback?
- Do you have experience managing multiple projects at once?
- Why should we hire you for this role?

---
  
## Section B: Full Interview / Core Competency Questions


### Project Planning & Execution
- Can you walk us through how you plan an eCommerce website project from kickoff to launch?
- How do you create and manage project timelines for web development projects?
- What tools do you use to build and track project plans?
- How do you estimate effort, timelines, and resources for an eCommerce project?
- How do you handle scope definition at the start of a project?
- What does a successful eCommerce launch look like to you?

### Budgeting & Resource Management
- How do you develop and manage a project budget?
- Have you ever had to deliver a project with limited resources? How did you handle it?
- How do you track expenses and ensure cost efficiency?
- What do you do if a project is at risk of going over budget?
- How do you prioritize features when budget constraints arise?

### Cross-Functional Team Coordination
- How do you coordinate work between designers, developers, and marketers?
- How do you manage task dependencies between teams?
- How do you resolve misalignment between stakeholders and delivery teams?
- How do you onboard new team members into an ongoing project?

### Risk Management & Problem Solving
- How do you identify risks early in an eCommerce project?
- Can you share an example of a project risk you mitigated?
- What do you do when a project falls behind schedule?
- How do you handle last-minute change requests?

### Quality & Client Expectations
- How do you ensure deliverables meet quality standards?
- What is your role in QA and UAT?
- How do you define “done”?
- How do you handle feedback that conflicts with the original scope?

### Stakeholder Communication
- How do you communicate project status to stakeholders?
- How do you deliver bad news or delays?
- What reporting methods do you typically use?

### Team Leadership & Conflict Resolution
- How do you foster collaboration?
- How do you resolve team conflict?
- How do you motivate teams during high-pressure periods?

### Tools, Methodologies & Best Practices
- What PM methodologies do you prefer?
- What tools have you used for managing web projects?
- How do you manage change control?

### Industry Awareness
- How do you stay current with eCommerce trends?
- What recent eCommerce trend excites or concerns you?
- What would you improve in your next project based on past experience?
